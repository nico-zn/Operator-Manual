Manage UsersÂ¶

The administration of your OpenJet plateform is accessible with the “Admin” role. Of course, you may want some features normally accessible by OPS or Sales (e.g. Pricing, Aircraft Performances) to be accessible only by an administrator. This is possible by changing the rights associated to the roles.

Contact support@openjet.com with your needs and we will do the necessary work.

This section will deal with the administration features that are commonly not accessible by OPS and Sales.
